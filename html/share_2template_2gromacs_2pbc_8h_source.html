00127 /* Calculate the correct distance vector from x2 to x1 and put it in dx.

00130  * For triclinic boxes pbc_dx does not necessarily return the shortest

00133  * have a shorter distance, but not shorter than sqrt(pbc->limit_distance2).
00134  * pbc->limit_distance2 is always larger than max_cutoff2(box).
00135  * For the standard rhombic dodecahedron and truncated octahedron
