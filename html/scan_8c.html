Do a partial reduce operation, based on rank: the results of the reduction operation of ranks 0 - i will be put in the recvbuf of rank i.

Collective function.

Parameters:
[in]sendbufThe operand parameters. All ranks may specify TMPI_IN_PLACE, in which case recvbuf will hold the operand parameters.
[in,out]recvbufThe result buffer.
[in]countThe number of items to do operation on.
[in]datatypeThe data type of the items.
[in]opThe operation to perform.
[in]commThe communicator.
Returns:
TMPI_SUCCESS on success, TMPI_FAILURE on failure.
